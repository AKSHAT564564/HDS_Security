Though people find cleanliness as their primary concern, the second most crucial problem is humans' second sense. Fix your fridge odour

The last night food is kept in the fridge. The smell originated from the same makes your fridge smell like a rotten warehouse, and the best way to avoid this smell is by using the well known smelling compound called vanilla extract. It is the compound used in ice creams and other various dishes to enhance the word's taste and smell. Take a cotton roll, dip it in water for some time, add some vanilla extract on it; take the role carefully, and place it in one corner of your fridge. After a few hours, when you open the refrigerator, it will smell like new and make you feel fresh. Remember to replace the cotton roll every 2-3 days to keep the smell going on and make the stinking smell of your fridge go away. Some other products can be used, like lemon, but the lemons' problems are that it can act strangely for the milk products, so being on the safer side prefers using the vanilla extract.
